VDC : BackOffice

Created by Stephan Oomen, last modified on 29 dec., 2021

The backoffice application (RegistratieXpertVdaInterface) is used by Monique and Jenny from VDC. From here they prepare the documents for vehicles that are requested for Export and BPM. It is connected to the DataConnectionWcf.

BPM

The batchroutine for BPM is every sunday, on friday the BPM gets done by the people from VDC and creates the required documents. This is done from the RegistratieXpertVdaInterface. It looks at the "tblBelastingHeader" table which vehicles are connected to the BPM for that week.

Every year

When a year passes, we need to update the LocatieVoorAangifte with the new year.
Simply change the year number e.g. 2018 to 2019
