8130 - Storage costs

Calculation is made in the Registratiexpert witch is base on the difference between booked in (20) (A) and booked out (120) (B) -5 days * 1,20 euro per dag

Example:

A = 01-02-2022
B = 10-02-2022

B-A = 10 days

10 days - 5 days = 5 days

5 days * 1,20 = € 6,-
