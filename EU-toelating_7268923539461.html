VDC : EU toelating

Created by Stephan Oomen on 04 feb., 2022

HOST: Requires the permission EU toelating in

When the vehicle is exported on BPM, it is possible to request an EU toelating

Go to vehicles and click on the vehicle. Then click on “EU toelating”.

The process is divided into 3 steps:

  • Choose KFZ- Zulassungsstelle

  • Verify documents

  • Send the documents to the KFZ- Zulassungsstelle

Choose KFZ- Zulassungsstelle

Here the application suggests the best location for the vehicle.

It is possible to set a default on Tenant level within Details to override the suggestion

Click on next, the documents required for the request will be generated.

Verify documents

A list of documents that needs to be reviewed, click on the document and review the details. Click on “verify” when this is OK.
After all documents are verified you are able to move on to the next step.

Send the documents to the KFZ- Zulassungsstelle

Now the email is being prepared and all documents that needs to be sent are included.

Review the details before sending. After submitting, the email will be sent and the vehicle will pushed to the status EU toelating aangevraagd

A notification will be sent to the Tenant that the status has changed
