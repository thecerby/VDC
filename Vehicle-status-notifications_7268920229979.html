VDC : Vehicle status notifications

Created by Stephan Oomen, last modified on 20 jan., 2022

VDC processes vehicles for their customers and can let the customer know when a status is changed on the vehicle. This status change triggers a notification towards the tenant directly in the application.

In order to enable this feature you need to opt in to the Vehicle status changes within the notification settings.

Go to the notification bell and click on the cog (settings)

There you enable the option “Vehicle status changes”

Hit changes and you are opted in!
